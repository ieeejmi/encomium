About Us

ENCOMIUM

Encomium, the intercollegiate annual techno-cultural fest of IEEE JMI chapter is an event with plethora of competitions lined up for participants. As the years progressed, it's dynamism and quest for perfection has attracted enthusiasts in large numbers. After our massive success last year, we are back, bigger, better and bolder, and we invite you to be a part of our annual extravaganza.

IEEE JMI

IEEE, Student branch, Jamia Millia Islamia, instituted twenty-five years ago in 1992, is one of most active technical societies in India today. It was one of the first student branches to be commissioned under the Delhi section. It forms an important part of the curriculum in engineering education and it's vitality is marked by the bridge it acts as, between students and professionals.

7000+ Footfall

With people stepping in from the entire DELHI, JMI is flooded with performers, enthusiasts and spectators to witness this flabbergasting fest. We don’t believe in bragging but a fest is a fest by the crowd it gathers and we stand high with 7000+ head count last year.

25+ Events

DANCE. DRAMA. LITERATURE. MUSIC. You name it, we have it. From competitions between enthusiastic participants to engaging the crowd, ENCOMIUM fest promises all. We have programs featuring artists which are sure to get you stunned.

25+ Sponsors

With more than 25 sponsors this year, we are confident to deliver what we promise and this year too ENCOMIUM would repeat history with its star events.
